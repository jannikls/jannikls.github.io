What I am thinking about

I am currently thinking about Germany's talent crisis, sacrifices, and classical fiction works.

  • On talent: I was researching the importance of human intelligence and spoke about my conclusions at ISIR 2023. I believe that the most talented people drive wealth creation and intellectual progress. Talent allocation of the most talented individuals is unusually ineffective, if progress is the main objective.
  • On advice: Good advice is incredibly rare. Giving and receiving advice requires good judgement. If you want to give better advice, ask yourself "What is going on with this human?".
  • On science: The replication crisis is real, and a significant share of our knowledge is wrong.
