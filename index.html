What I am currently thinking about:

  • On talent: I believe that the most talented people drive wealth creation and intellectual progress. Besides exceptional intelligence, drive and bias to action seem to be strong predictors of achieving eminence. Talent allocation of the most talented individuals is unusually ineffective, if progress is the main objective.
  • On advice: Good advice is incredibly rare. Giving and receiving advice requires good judgement. If you want to give better advice, ask yourself "What is going on with this human?".
  • On science: The replication crisis is real, and a significant share of our knowledge is wrong.
